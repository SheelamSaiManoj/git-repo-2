Food Waste Management

Our primary goal is to build a sustainable and collaborative community by reducing food waste, optimizing resource utilization among students, and fostering a sense of environmental responsibility. EcoConnect aims to bridge the gap between excess resources and unmet needs, creating a win-win situation for both the environment and the student lifestyle
